This project was created for the purpose of showing potential employers my work. The idea was to create a very simple and elegant portfolio, yet colorful. The colors are some of my favorite pastel colors and represent the bubbly side of myself. This project also allowed me to try out Slick.js for the first time, a fully responsive & flexible jQuery carousel plugin. The images shown are mockups of the website that I created before development, and the color scheme I chose.
