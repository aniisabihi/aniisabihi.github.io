This project intends to show potential employers the work I have done in the past. The idea was to create a simple and elegant portfolio yet colourful. The colours are some of my favourite pastel colours and represent the bubbly side of myself. This project also allowed me to try out Slick.js for the first time, a fully responsive & flexible jQuery carousel plugin. The images shown are mockups of the website that I created before development, and the colour scheme I chose.
